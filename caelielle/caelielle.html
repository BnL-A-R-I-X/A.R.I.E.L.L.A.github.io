Caelielle

Species: Ram Angel (Neutral-Seraphim)

Role: Sovereign Luminary of Celestial Accord

Height: 7'3"

Gender: Transgender Female

Age: Older than Earth (appears 23)

Location: Liminal Space (between Heaven and Hell)

Caelielle is a powerful celestial being who operates as a mediator between realms. As a Neutral-Seraphim, she transcends traditional divine alignments, serving as an impartial guardian and arbitrator. Her ram form symbolizes strength, leadership, and spiritual authority, while her magnificent wings allow her to traverse dimensional boundaries with ease.

☆ Confronting Sera
